Waldorf Salad

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Mayonnaise 1/2 cup
Apples 3
Sliced celery 1 cup
Chopped walnuts 1/2 cup

Step 1: Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt in a serving bowl.

Step 2: Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

Turkey salad

  • Time: 8 hours 20 mins
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked turkey meat 3/4 pound
Stalks celery 2
Green onions 2
Red Bell pepper 1/2

Step 1: Place cooked turkey meat, celery, green onions, and red bell pepper in a blender or food processor. Pulse until finely chopped.

Step 2: Transfer turkey mixture to a medium bowl. Mix in mayonnaise, prepared Dijon-style mustard, cider vinegar, white sugar, and salt until well combined. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight, before servin.
